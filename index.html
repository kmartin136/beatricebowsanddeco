ABOUT

 Kathryn "Kat" Martin is the owner of Beatrice Bows and Deco owned and operated since 2018. She is a veteran and has honorably served for 20 years in the United States Army. While serving she also gained an MBA and multiple other certifications. Business has its advantages, but she wanted a way to creatively express herself and spend more time with her husband, Michael, 6 kids and 2 dogs. Her focus on making others smile through her creative talents is what its all about and enhancing . Her goal is to work with all children and adults, helping them express their lives through all creative art platforms.
